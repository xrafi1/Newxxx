Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you make a donation, sign up for our newsletter, or contact us, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details. Your payment information is securely processed by our payment partners and is not stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process your donations, send you tax receipts, provide updates on the campaigns you supported, and respond to your inquiries. We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. All sensitive payment data is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and encrypted into our payment gateway provider's database, accessible only by authorized personnel.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@kindfund.org.
